about us

Unveiling the Essence of Premium Dust Tea

HazloTea, a distinguished purveyor of premium Indian teas, invites you on a sensory journey that transcends ordinary tea experiences. Nestled in the heart of Naripparambu, Malappuram, Kerala, HazloTea meticulously curates the finest tea powders from ten renowned estates across India, including Assam, Munnar, Idukki, Wayanad, and Nilgiri. With a legacy spanning four decades, our esteemed owner, a revered government employee and accomplished tea taster, leads a dedicated team committed to delivering an unparalleled tea experience. Our teas, a true testament to Indian craftsmanship, blend the essence of these diverse varieties into a product that captures the spirit of our nation.

At HazloTea, quality is not just a commitment; it's a tradition. Our own unit in Naripparambu, Ponnani, equipped with all necessary licenses, ensures the highest standards of quality and purity. With a range of 50 unique tea products, crafted without the need for trademark registration, our state-of-the-art godown preserves the freshness of our teas, offering a one-year expiry that resonates with our discerning customers. HazloTea is not merely a brand; it symbolizes trust, sourcing teas directly from government-approved channels, guaranteeing authenticity and a pesticide-free experience. Embark on an unparalleled tea journey with HazloTea, where tradition meets innovation, and every cup is a celebration of India's finest teas, crafted with passion, expertise, and a commitment to excellence.
